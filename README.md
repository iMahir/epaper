# E-Paper Collection

E-Paper Collection is a serverless digital newspaper aggregator built with Cloudflare Workers. It scrapes the latest e-paper PDF links from multiple newspaper sites and presents them in a modern, responsive interface.

## Available Papers

- The Times of India
- The Hindu
- The New Indian Express
- The Telegraph
- The Economic Times
- The Tribune
- The Statesman
- Business Standard
- Financial Express
- Free Press Journal
- Mint
- The Asian Age
- Deccan Chronicle
- The Pioneer
